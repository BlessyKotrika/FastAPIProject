# KhetiPulse: Presentation Flow & Script

This document provides a slide-by-slide flow for a formal presentation of the KhetiPulse project. It is designed to explain the "Why", "What", and "How" of the platform for stakeholders and technical judges.

---

## 📽️ Slide-by-Slide Presentation Flow

### **Slide 1: Title & Vision**
*   **Content**: Project Name (KhetiPulse), Tagline ("Precision AI for Every Farmer"), Your Name/Team.
*   **The Hook**: "Imagine a world where a farmer's smartphone is as powerful as an agronomist's degree. That's KhetiPulse."

### **Slide 2: Why this Project? (The Mission)**
*   **Key Question**: Why build KhetiPulse?
*   **Content**: 
    *   Agriculture is the world's most vital industry but faces the most unpredictability.
    *   **Purpose**: To provide a unified, intelligent decision support system.
    *   **The Goal**: Turn data into yield and yield into prosperity.

### **Slide 3: Why is it Important? (The Problem)**
*   **Key Question**: Why does this matter today?
*   **Content**:
    *   **Climate Change**: Extreme weather events are destroying crops at an unprecedented rate.
    *   **Market Volatility**: Farmers lose up to 30% of their potential income due to poor market timing.
    *   **Complexity**: Accessing government subsidies and expert advice is often a bureaucratic nightmare.
    *   **Impact**: Financial stability for 140 million farming families in India alone.

### **Slide 4: What are the Core Features? (The Solution)**
*   **Key Question**: What does KhetiPulse actually do?
*   **Content**:
    1.  **AI Advisor (RAG Chat)**: 24/7 expert advice based on verified agricultural knowledge.
    2.  **Smart Mandi Insights**: Live price tracking and predictive selling recommendations.
    3.  **Weather Pulse**: Hyper-local, farm-specific alerts and action plans.
    4.  **Scheme Discovery**: Instant matching with government subsidies and grants.

### **Slide 5: Why is AI Important Here? (The Intelligence)**
*   **Key Question**: Why not just a regular app?
*   **Content**:
    *   **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**: We don't just use a generic chatbot. Our AI (Claude 3 on AWS Bedrock) reads specific, verified agricultural documents to give safe, accurate advice.
    *   **Contextual Understanding**: The AI knows the farmer's crop, soil type, and location, providing tailored "prescriptions" rather than generic "tips."
    *   **Scalability**: AI allows one system to provide expert-level guidance to millions of farmers simultaneously.

### **Slide 6: Technical Architecture (The Engine)**
*   **Content**:
    *   **Frontend**: Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS (Modern, Mobile-First).
    *   **Backend**: FastAPI (Async, High-Performance Python).
    *   **AI/Infrastructure**: AWS Bedrock (Claude 3), DynamoDB, and ECS.
    *   **Diagram Reference**: Refer to the `architecture_diagram.html` in the project root.

### **Slide 7: Live Demo / User Journey**
*   **Flow**:
    *   **Onboarding**: Showing how we capture farm data.
    *   **Dashboard**: The central hub for weather and prices.
    *   **Smart Selling**: Finding the best price in a nearby Mandi.
    *   **AI Advisor**: Asking a specific question like "How do I treat blight on my tomatoes?"

### **Slide 8: Innovation & Security**
*   **Content**:
    *   **Dual-Auth System**: Local credentials + Google OAuth2 for accessibility.
    *   **Performance**: Shared HTTP clients and optimized dependency injection for sub-second responses.
    *   **Robustness**: Standardized error handling ensures the app works even when external APIs fail.

### **Slide 9: Future Roadmap**
*   **Content**:
    *   Multi-lingual voice support (Vernacular languages).
    *   IoT sensor integration (Soil moisture, pH sensors).
    *   Satellite-based crop health monitoring.

### **Slide 10: Conclusion & Q&A**
*   **Final Thought**: "KhetiPulse isn't just an app; it's a lifeline for the modern farmer."
*   **Action**: Invite questions and direct them to the interactive `openapi.json` for technical details.
